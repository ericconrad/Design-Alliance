Deanna Marino

I was born in Fayetteville, North Carolina and currently living in Harford County, Maryland. I’m a part time high school senior and part time college freshman. Freelance graphic design/digital artist (founding my company Deanna Joy Marino Works) specializing in Advertising Design. I have employed my skills most recently directing three full- scale theatrical productions and curating local art exhibitions. My hobbies include seeing movies and plays and I collect buttons and feathers!
